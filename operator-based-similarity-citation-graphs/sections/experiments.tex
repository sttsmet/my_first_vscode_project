\section{Experiments}
\label{sec:experiments}

This section can be expanded into a full experimental evaluation. A reasonable starting
protocol is:
\begin{enumerate}
    \item Build a citation graph from a benchmark scholarly dataset.
    \item Compare local, path-based, and diffusion operators at the article level.
    \item Aggregate article similarity into researcher similarity with several
    normalization rules.
    \item Evaluate article retrieval and researcher retrieval separately.
\end{enumerate}

Key metrics may include precision at $k$, normalized discounted cumulative gain, and rank
correlation against expert-labeled or metadata-derived relevance judgments.
